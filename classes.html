ICT Classes for O/L and A/L Students

Our classes are designed to help students succeed in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) at both Ordinary Level (O/L) and Advanced Level (A/L). The lessons combine theory, practical activities, and exam preparation to ensure every student clearly understands each topic.

What We Offer

📘 O/L ICT Classes

  • Complete syllabus coverage
  • Easy explanations of ICT concepts
  • Practical computer activities
  • Preparation for O/L examinations

💻 A/L ICT Classes

  • In-depth understanding of ICT subjects
  • Advanced theory and practical training
  • Exam-focused learning
  • Guidance for achieving high results

Practical Learning

Students learn not only theory but also hands-on computer practicals. Practical sessions help students understand how ICT works in real-life situations.

Weekly and Unit Tests

To monitor student progress, weekly papers revise lessons and unit papers are given after completing each unit to ensure exam readiness.

Individual Student Attention

The teacher’s dedication ensures every child receives personal guidance to gain confidence and clear any doubts in ICT.

A Friendly and Supportive Learning Environment

A positive space where students can Ask questions freely, Improve their knowledge, and Build confidence.
