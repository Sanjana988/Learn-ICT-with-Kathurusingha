BIOGRAPHY

I am Jude Kathurusingha, a passionate and visionary ICT educator dedicated to guiding students toward success in the rapidly evolving digital world. I hold a BSc (Hons) in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science and an Advanced Diploma in Information and Communication Technology specializing in Business IT from MDIS University, Singapore. I have also completed a Diploma in Network Engineering from SLIIT, which has strengthened my knowledge in both theoretical and practical aspects of modern technology.

I have gained valuable experience working as a former lecturer and as an Advanced Level ICT teacher at a leading international school. Through these roles, I have developed a strong passion for teaching ICT in a clear, concept-based, and practical manner that helps students truly understand technology rather than simply memorize it.As the Founder of ICT with Kathurusingha and the CEO of Istharam Online Education, my aim is to make ICT education more accessible, engaging, and practical for students. My focus is on transforming complex technological concepts into simple and actionable skills using real-world examples and modern digital learning methods.
